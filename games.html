Mobile Legends: Bang Bang





       Moonton, a division of ByteDance, created and published Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game for mobile devices. After its 2016 release, the game quickly gained traction, particularly in Southeast Asia.

Stardew Valley

       Was created by Eric "ConcernedApe" Barone and is a role-playing simulation game. In Stardew Valley, the player assumes the role of a young person who has just inherited the family farm from his or her grandfather. In February of 2016, the game was made available for the Windows platform. You can do just about anything in Stardew Valley, from cultivating crops and tending to livestock to fishing, cooking, mining, foraging, and socializing with the locals to getting married and starting a family. It supports online co-op play for a maximum of four players.

My Time at Portia





       Move to the charming city of Portia and begin a new life! Rebuild your late grandfather's once-proud workshop by taking on jobs, tending to crops and livestock, and making friends with the interesting locals of this charming post-apocalyptic world. You set out to become the best workshop in Portia using your grandfather's old guidebook and workbench. Restore the town and learn its secrets by working with the locals. However, you should know that it won't be simple. You'll find lots of nice people to talk to in Portia. Get to know people, do favors for them, give and receive presents, go on dates, and watch your budding romance unfold! My Time at Portia, like all Studio Ghibli films, will transport you to a wondrous world you won't soon forget.

Pokemon Games

       Game Freak created the Pokémon video game series, which Nintendo and The Pokémon Company publish as part of the Pokémon media franchise. Developed by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori, the series debuted in 1996 with Pocket Monsters Red and Green for the Game Boy in Japan and as Pokémon Red and Blue elsewhere. The "core series" of role-playing games (RPGs) has continued on every Nintendo handheld system since the original Game Boy.





Legend of Zelda





       Japanese video game designers Shigeru Miyamoto and Takashi Tezuka are responsible for the long-running The Legend of Zelda franchise. The series is primarily created and published by Nintendo, though Flagship, Vanpool, and Grezzo have worked on portable installments and re-releases. The gameplay combines elements from both action RPGs and action-adventure games.
